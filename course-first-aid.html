First Aid

Course Fee: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Course Purpose

This comprehensive First Aid course is designed to equip learners with essential life-saving skills and emergency response knowledge. Participants will gain the confidence and competence to handle medical emergencies in various settings, making them valuable assets in domestic and workplace environments.

Course Content

  • Basic life support and CPR techniques
  • Wound care and bandaging
  • Burns and scalds treatment
  • Choking response procedures
  • Shock recognition and management
  • Fracture and sprain care
  • Emergency scene assessment
  • Legal aspects of first aid
  • Use of first aid equipment
  • Practical scenario training

What You'll Gain

  • Nationally recognized First Aid certificate
  • Increased employability in domestic and care sectors
  • Confidence to handle emergency situations
  • Enhanced personal and family safety skills
  • Professional development opportunities
