Sewing

Course Fee: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Course Purpose

Learn comprehensive sewing and tailoring skills to create, alter, and repair clothing and textiles. This course provides both hand-sewing and machine-sewing techniques, enabling learners to start their own tailoring business or enhance their employment prospects in the textile industry.

Course Content

  • Basic hand-sewing techniques
  • Sewing machine operation and maintenance
  • Pattern reading and cutting
  • Garment construction techniques
  • Fabric selection and care
  • Measuring and fitting
  • Alterations and repairs
  • Embroidery and decorative stitching
  • Business skills for tailoring
  • Quality control and finishing

What You'll Gain

  • Professional sewing and tailoring certificate
  • Skills to start your own tailoring business
  • Employment opportunities in textile industry
  • Ability to create and alter clothing
  • Additional income generation skills
