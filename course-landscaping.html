Landscaping

Course Fee: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Course Purpose

Master the art and science of landscaping, from garden design to plant care. This course provides comprehensive training in creating and maintaining beautiful outdoor spaces, making graduates highly sought-after in the gardening and landscaping industry.

Course Content

  • Garden design principles
  • Plant identification and selection
  • Soil analysis and improvement
  • Irrigation and drainage systems
  • Tree and shrub planting techniques
  • Lawn establishment and maintenance
  • Pest and disease management
  • Seasonal garden maintenance
  • Landscaping tools and equipment
  • Client consultation skills

What You'll Gain

  • Professional landscaping certificate
  • Skills to start landscaping business
  • Employment in gardening services
  • Knowledge of sustainable gardening practices
  • Enhanced property maintenance abilities
