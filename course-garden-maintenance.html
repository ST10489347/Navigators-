Garden Maintenance

Course Fee: R750

Duration: 6 Weeks

Course Purpose

Learn essential garden care and maintenance techniques to keep outdoor spaces beautiful and healthy. This practical course covers everything from plant care to seasonal maintenance, making graduates valuable in domestic and commercial gardening roles.

Course Content

  • Plant care and watering techniques
  • Pruning and trimming methods
  • Lawn care and maintenance
  • Weed control and prevention
  • Seasonal garden tasks
  • Composting and organic gardening
  • Garden tool use and maintenance
  • Pest identification and control
  • Planting and transplanting
  • Garden safety practices

What You'll Gain

  • Garden maintenance certificate
  • Employment in gardening services
  • Skills for landscaping support roles
  • Knowledge for personal garden care
  • Seasonal maintenance expertise
