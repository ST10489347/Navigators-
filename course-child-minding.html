Child Minding

Course Fee: R750

Duration: 6 Weeks

Course Purpose

Learn professional childcare skills and child development knowledge to provide quality care for children. This course covers essential aspects of child minding, from safety and nutrition to developmental activities and behavior management.

Course Content

  • Child development stages
  • Child safety and first aid
  • Nutrition and meal planning
  • Age-appropriate activities
  • Behavior management techniques
  • Communication with parents
  • Emergency procedures
  • Creating safe environments
  • Educational play activities
  • Professional childcare ethics

What You'll Gain

  • Child minding certificate
  • Employment in childcare services
  • Skills to start childcare business
  • Enhanced parenting knowledge
  • Professional development in care sector
