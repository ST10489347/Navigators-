Cooking

Course Fee: R750

Duration: 6 Weeks

Course Purpose

Master culinary skills and nutrition knowledge for professional cooking environments. This course provides practical cooking techniques, menu planning, and food safety knowledge essential for domestic and commercial cooking positions.

Course Content

  • Basic cooking techniques and methods
  • Knife skills and food preparation
  • Nutrition and healthy meal planning
  • Food safety and hygiene
  • International cuisine basics
  • Baking and pastry fundamentals
  • Menu planning and costing
  • Kitchen management
  • Dietary requirements and allergies
  • Professional cooking standards

What You'll Gain

  • Professional cooking certificate
  • Employment in domestic cooking roles
  • Catering business opportunities
  • Enhanced family nutrition skills
  • Food service industry readiness
